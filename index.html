IEEE BUP SB WIE Affinity Group aspires to work with the engineering community to share content and information that illuminates future innovators. And more importantly,to show how they can be part of the solutions to some of the toughest challenges facing society by becoming an engineer.

The mission of IEEE BUP SB WIE Affinity Group is to raise the profiles of our remarkable female engineers and encourage those who aspire to become engineers.We believe that by narrowing the gender gap in the fields of Engineering and technology we can transform the future of humanity.

About Us

IEEE BUP SB WIE Affinity has started its journey from November 9, 2019 [Officially] with a number of active participations and achievements. It is trying to build a powerful message that a girl can be both a mother(bringing upon a child) and an engineer(inventing a machine).

We want to inspire and promote women engineers and enthusiasts to follow their academic interests and build a career in engineering. We ask them to come forward to show their worth in innovation, technical skills and engineering for a better world. But as for inspiration, the men are also a bright example of encouragement in showing how great each aspect of modern tech and engineering can be. With further words, they are also a mark of this inspiration to women and we welcome them to join in WIE Affinity affiliated activities.

Thus, we embrace the effort of both female and male here to provide their accomplishments through science, creativity and innovation with a look to serve humanity.

IEEE WIE Affinity Group

An IEEE affinity group is a local unit of an IEEE organizational unit or standing committee to fulfill the purposes of IEEE.

IEEE Women in Engineering (WIE) is one of the largest international professional organizations that advocate women in leadership roles in IEEE governance and career advancement for women in the profession.IEEE WIE members make lifelong friendships, acquire influential mentors, and make a difference for the benefit of humanity.

IEEE WIE

Become an IEEE WIE Member

Join a global network of IEEE members who have an interest in promoting women engineers and scientists, and inspiring girls around the world to follow their academic interests in a career in engineering and science.

How to Join?

IEEE WIE membership is offered to all IEEE members. IEEE WIE membership is free for Students, Graduate Student Members, and Life Members. Select “IEEE Women in Engineering Membership” under “Special Interest Groups” when you join or renew online.
